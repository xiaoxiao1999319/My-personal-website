Research Summary & Technical Skills

Research Summary

My research focuses on the intersection of energy storage/harvesting, sensing technologies, and bioinspired programmable materials for healthcare and sustainability.

Technical Skills

  • Battery Assembly and Test (glove box, electrochemical workstation, battery tester)
  • Fabrication (Proto UV laser, CO2 laser, 3D printer, spin coating, plating, freeze-drying)
  • Materials Characterization (wettability test, mechanical test, confocal microscopy, SEM, XRD)
  • Electrical Characterization (LabView, NI-DAQs, function generator, oscilloscope)
  • Circuitry Design and Soldering (ESP32, nRF52, soldering under a microscope)
  • Embedded Development and Coding (Arduino, Python, C, MATLAB)
  • Simulation (fluid/solid mechanics, electromagnetism, EM metamaterials, antenna)
